About The Company

Welcome to Elevate Abroad, where your global dreams take flight. More than an education consultancy, we’re your trusted partner in building a future shaped by your goals and aspirations.

Why Choose Us

  1. We shape your future through your eyes

    Having worked extensively in the education sector, we have seen how many consulting firms prioritize business over students. This is where Elevate Abroad stands apart. We focus on your goals, challenges, and success. Our approach is student-first, ensuring you receive guidance based on your strengths, interests, and career aspirations.

  2. Personalized & Trustworthy Support

    At Elevate Abroad, we do not believe in a one-size-fits-all approach. We work directly with students, providing one-on-one mentoring rather than passing applications to external consultants. With direct access through texts, calls, and personal guidance, we ensure that every step of your application journey is carefully crafted for success.

  1. Backed by Expertise & Real World Insights

    With deep experience in career guidance, application strategy, and mentorship, we stay updated with real-time insights from universities and industries. Whether it’s selecting the right course, crafting a compelling application, or preparing for life abroad, we help you navigate every challenge with confidence.

  2. Proven Track Record of Success

    At Elevate Abroad, your dream is our mission. From securing top university admits to guiding professionals toward the right career path, we’re known for delivering results that matter. Your goals drive us, and exceeding expectations is our standard. We see the journey through your eyes and we are here to help you make it a reality.

Our Process

Discover How We Work

We tailor our support to your unique goals and needs, treating each application as if it were our own.

Profile Evaluation & University Shortlisting

We shortlist a combination of dream, target, and safe universities depending on your goals, cultural fit, prior experiences, and potential add-ups to your profile!

FAQ’s

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Elevate Abroad?

Elevate Abroad is a boutique study abroad consultancy that helps students navigate the study abroad process from university selection to application guidance. We assist with applications for UG, MBA, MiM, Master’s, and PhD programs.

Which programs and countries do you support?

We assist with applications for Undergraduate, MIM, MBA, Master’s, and PhD programs. Our students apply to top universities in the US, UK, Canada, Germany, Spain, Europe, and Singapore. Additionally, we also help students with applications to top universities and institutions in India, ensuring they have access to the best opportunities both domestically and internationally.

Do you provide GMAT/GRE/IELTS/TOEFL/SAT preparation?

Yes! We help identify scholarship opportunities, craft compelling essays, and guide you through financial aid applications.

Do you provide standalone services to students?

Yes, we offer a range of standalone services, including application review, resume/CV editing, LinkedIn profile polishing, and comprehensive essay feedback. Whether you need full guidance or just support on specific components, we tailor our services to your needs.

How is Elevate Abroad different from other consultancies?

At Elevate Abroad, we do not believe in a one-size-fits-all approach. We work directly with students, providing one-on-one mentoring rather than passing applications to external consultants. With direct access through texts, calls, and personal guidance, we ensure that every step of your application journey is carefully crafted for success.

Do you guarantee university admissions?

No one can guarantee admissions, but our expert guidance and strategic approach significantly enhance your chances of success.

Which degrees do you assist with?

We specialize in applications for MBA, MiM (Master’s in Management), Master’s, Deferred MBA, and PhD programs across top global universities.

Do you assist with visa applications?

Yes! We have trusted visa service partners who provide end-to-end assistance, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free visa application process.
