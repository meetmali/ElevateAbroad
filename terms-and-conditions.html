1. General Disclaimer

Elevate Abroad is an independent education consultancy offering personalized guidance for global and Indian higher education. We are not affiliated with any university or college, and we do not guarantee admissions, interviews, or scholarships at any institution. Our advice is based on experience and best practices, but results depend on the student's profile, effort, and external factors beyond our control.


2. Services & Scope


  • Application Guidance: Includes personalized counseling, university shortlisting, application planning, essay editing, and interview preparation. Final responsibility for content accuracy, submission, and deadlines lies with the applicant.
  • Visa Support: While Elevate Abroad provides general guidance and mock preparation for visa interviews, we do not offer legal visa assistance. Applicants must follow up with relevant consulates and embassies.
  • Test Prep: Elevate Abroad does not offer in-house test prep (SAT, GRE, GMAT, TOEFL, IELTS), but we may guide students on timelines, resources, and strategies.
  • Recommender Support: We help students frame recommendation strategies but do not directly interact with recommenders. The applicant is responsible for coordination and timely submission.
  • Scholarship & Financial Aid Advice: We provide assistance with scholarship essays and offer advice on potential funding options, but securing financial aid remains the student's responsibility.
  • Mentorship Programs: Offered selectively, these involve in-depth one-on-one sessions. Packages are designed for specific timelines and cannot be extended beyond agreed periods.
