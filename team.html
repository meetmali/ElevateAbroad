Deepa Raju Fatnani

Deepa Raju Fatnani founded Elevate Abroad with a clear mission to provide ethical, student-first mentorship for studying abroad. Having once aspired to study abroad but been unable to do so due to family commitments, she understands the challenges students face firsthand. With years of experience in the education sector, she identified a critical gap in the industry, where many consulting firms prioritized business interests over truly guiding students toward the right opportunities. Seeing students struggle with complex applications, misleading promises, and a lack of personalized support, she was determined to create a solution built on transparency and genuine guidance.

Elevate Abroad was founded to offer personalized, goal-oriented mentorship based on each student’s aspirations, background, and budget. Unlike transactional consulting firms, it provides dedicated support from university selection to visas, with a strong focus on integrity and student success. More than a consultancy, Elevate Abroad is a trusted partner in every student’s global education journey.

Target Top Universities With E.A

Applying to top universities requires careful attention to multiple key components.

  • A Well Curated CV

    With years of experience in the education consulting space, we know exactly what top universities and adcom value in a CV: clarity, relevance, structure, and strong, purposeful language. We don’t just enhance your existing CV; we completely transform it into a compelling document that effectively showcases your academic journey, skills, achievements, and aspirations.

  • Compelling and Personalized LORs

    Different universities have different preferences, some prioritize academic letters of recommendation, while others value professional ones. Drawing insights from successful applicants at top universities around the world, we provide end-to-end support in crafting impactful and personalized LORs.

  • Powerful and Purposeful Essays

    Essays are often the most critical part of your application. Unlike many who just polish grammar, we focus on what truly matters your story. Most applicants struggle because traditional counselors lack insight into what admissions committees actually look for. That’s one of the key reasons we started this consultancy.
